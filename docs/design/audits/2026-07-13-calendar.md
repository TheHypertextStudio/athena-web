## Findings (ordered by severity)

1. **P2 — The global recovery-code reminder consumes meaningful vertical space on a 390px
   calendar.** The calendar itself reflows correctly and remains operable, but the banner leaves
   fewer immediately visible hours in both narrow screenshots. Consider allowing the global shell
   reminder to collapse after its first acknowledged display; this is outside the calendar
   component and does not block this surface.
2. **P3 — The empty Layers section relies on the nearby New action rather than embedding another
   button.** Its copy names both valid next steps and duplicating New would weaken the one-primary-
   action hierarchy, so retain this unless user testing shows the relationship is missed.

Verdict: **SHIP** — all eight dimensions meet the crafted bar and all five hard gates are green.
